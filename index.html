Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to set up the bot and start growing your Discord server

1

Invite the Bot

Add the bot to your Discord server with the necessary permissions for channel management and message sending.

Required Permissions: Send Messages, Embed Links, Read Message History, Use Slash Commands
2

Run Initial Setup

Use the /setup command to configure your partner channel, bump channel, categories, and log channel.

/setup partner_channel:#partnerships bump_channel:#advertising categories:Gaming log_channel:#bot-logs
3

Understanding Categories

Categories help match your server with relevant partners. Choose from: Community, Gaming, Education, Technology, Arts, Music, Sports, or Other. This affects how the bot networks with other servers.

4

Configure Channels

Partner Channel: Where partnership requests from other servers will be posted
Bump Channel: Where your server ads will be sent to other servers
Log Channel: Where bot activities and notifications will be logged

5

Test Your Setup

Try your first bump with /bump to ensure everything is working correctly!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the bot

What happens after I bump my server?

After bumping, your server advertisement is sent to other servers' bump channels based on category matching. You'll receive a confirmation message with the next available bump time (usually 2 hours for free users, faster for premium).

How do I redeem premium features?

Run /settings and click the "Activate Premium" button. You can also earn premium for free by voting 30 times using /claimvote to track your progress.

What if I have issues with setup?

Join our support server for help, or use /help for command assistance. Make sure the bot has proper permissions: Send Messages, Embed Links, Read Message History, and Use Slash Commands.

Can I transfer premium to another server?

Premium features are typically server-specific. Contact support through our Discord server if you need assistance with premium transfers or have special circumstances.

How long does premium last?

Premium duration varies by how it was obtained. Vote-based premium typically lasts 30 days. Check /settings to see your current premium status and expiration date.

Why am I not receiving partnership requests?

Ensure your server is properly set up with /setup, your category is selected correctly, and your partner channel has proper permissions. Partnership requests are matched based on categories and server activity.
